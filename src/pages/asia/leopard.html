Common name: Leopard

Scientific name: Panthera pardus

The Leopard is a large cat native to various regions of Africa and Asia.

About Leopards

Leopards are adaptable and highly elusive predators, known for their spotted fur and stealthy hunting abilities.

They inhabit a variety of habitats, including forests, savannas, and mountainous regions.

Conservation Status

The Leopard is classified as Near Threatened by the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN).

Threats to their survival include habitat loss, poaching for their fur and bones, and conflict with humans.
